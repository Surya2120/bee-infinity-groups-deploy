Who We Are

Bee Infinity Groups is a multi-vertical creative organization delivering premium solutions across film production, event management, and dance choreography. We believe in creating win-win collaborations that ensure mutual growth and long-term relationships, while keeping our services accessible and valuable.

Built on passion, discipline, and experience, we focus on turning creative ideas into impactful visual and experiential realities. Our approach blends artistic creativity with professional execution. While we are a business, our vision goes beyond profit — we are committed to building a strong creative community that supports, inspires, and grows together.

Our Journey

Built through resilience. Driven by vision.

“Every setback refined the vision. Bee Infinity Groups was never built on comfort, it was built on resilience.”

Where It All Began

It started with a big dream but no clear plan.

At 19, I took my first real step and started my own company.

In the beginning, I thought everything had to be perfect before I could begin. But I realized something perfection is just another form of procrastination. So I started anyway.

My parents have been my strongest support from day one and they continue to stand by me even now. Their belief in me has been the strength behind every step I’ve taken.

The journey hasn’t been easy. There were setbacks, financial losses, and moments when people walked away during the most important phases of growth.

I learned. I failed. I got back up. And I failed again. But I never stopped.

I’m still building, growing, improving, and chasing the vision I started with. With limited resources and no clear direction, I kept moving forward step by step.

Today, I have clarity. I know where I’m going and I’m building towards it with purpose.

This is just the beginning of something much bigger.

Because the truth is simple: you don’t wait to be perfect to exist… you exist first, and then you become.

— Surya SharavanaFounder, Bee Infinity Groups

From Student to Mentor

Stepped into teaching, mentoring students at the very institute where the journey began — marking the transformation from student to leader.

2017

First Studio Launch

Founded “Spirit Radiant Dance Academy,” launching the first studio space with support from family and collaborators. Later rebranded as “Shaivam – The School of Dance and Arts.”

2018

Independent Expansion

Continued independently, expanded classes across locations, delivered performances, and executed contract projects while celebrating the first milestone year.

2019

International Qualification

Qualified for an international dance competition in Portugal. The experience reshaped long-term vision and expansion into events and film execution.
